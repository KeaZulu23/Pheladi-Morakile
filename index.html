Pheladi Morakile has been in the financial industry for more than 18 years. Life coaching is her purpose and she is passionate about growth, personal development, purpose alignment and helping people to transform from their limited beliefs mindset and to start flourishing in their world.

Values and Philosophy

My coaching philosophy is to promote growth and transformation in individuals at all levels by empowering them to recognise their own hidden potential and overcome their limiting beliefs and make a meaningful impact in their space.

​I create a safe and non-judgemental environment whereby facing your limiting beliefs is encouraged with confidentiality being a priority. I utilise a thought provoking processes that inspires individuals to maximise their professional and personal potential. My approaches and tools are customised for the change to be sustainable. I encourage open and honest dialogues as this will allow us to analyse and solution for the root cause. My role is to facilitate the growth and transformational process and this is achieved by discipline and accountability by the coach. ​Constant reflection as a coach is key in my practice as that contributes to my continuous development.

I commit to serve my clients in a respectable and professional manner and put them first by listening attentively to what is being said and the unspoken words. My purpose is to have a positive impact and empower them in their journey.
